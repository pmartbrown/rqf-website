What EMD funding is

We wire your earnest money deposit to escrow so you can lock the contract without pulling from your own reserves. Built for high-velocity wholesalers and Gator-style operators juggling multiple contracts at once.

When to use it

  • You're juggling multiple contracts and need EMD liquidity
  • A deadline is hours away and your capital is deployed elsewhere
  • You want to keep dry powder available for the deals that need it

How RealQuick Funds funds it

01Submit the contract, EMD amount, and escrow details.
02Fast verification and written terms - typically same business day.
03EMD wires to escrow; repayment per the agreement at closing or termination.

What you'll need

  • Executed purchase contract
  • Escrow/title wiring instructions
  • Clear EMD refundability terms
